## Instrument Training
---
I provide training for students and junior researchers on atmospheric measurement techniques and data analysis. The training covers instrument principles, operation, calibration, quality control, and data interpretation, with a focus on online aerosol and trace-gas measurements. <br>

Training topics include: <br>
- Principles and applications of aerosol mass spectrometry and chemical ionization mass spectrometry, e.g., ACSM, AMS, FIGAERO-CIMS <br>
- Operation, calibration, and quality control of online aerosol measurement instruments and related data analysis<br>
- Source apportionment of organic aerosol <br>
